How it works

The complete journey, end to end.

Lakshya covers the entire lifecycle — from the moment a learner walks in to the day they receive their first salary.

1
Register & onboard
Learners create a profile through voice or text in Hindi, English, or regional languages. AI captures background, goals, and preferences to personalise the experience.
2
Choose a skill track
AI recommends the most relevant course from the catalogue based on the learner's profile and local job market demand. Learner selects and begins.
3
Train, assess, and grow
Complete structured learning modules with AI-driven assessments. Weekly progress is tracked and visible to trainers and program managers.
4
Get certified
On completion, learners receive a verifiable digital credential. AI instantly generates an ATS-optimised resume. Mock interviews prepare them for employer conversations.
5
Match to a job & get placed
The AI jobs engine surfaces nearby openings ranked by compatibility score. Employers review profiles, shortlist, and schedule interviews — all on Disha.
